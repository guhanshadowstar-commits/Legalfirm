About The Firm

Advocate K. Shankar

Founder & Managing Advocate

Prudent Legal Services was founded by Adv. Shankar and his associates, who have spent more than a decade practising before civil, criminal and registration courts. What began as an individual practice has grown into a firm supported by associate advocates handling matters across multiple areas of law.

Every brief — whether a contract, a contested inheritance, or a corporate compliance question — is approached the same way: read closely, researched thoroughly, and explained in plain language before any step is taken.

  • Enrolled, Bar Council of Tamil Nadu & Puducherry
  • Practising before civil, criminal & registration courts
  • Advises individuals, families, builders and growing businesses
  • Legal opinions delivered in a structured, written format
